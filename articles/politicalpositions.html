political positions

articles contain much more text then other pages, and as such they are in dark mode for less intensive viewing

It should be noted that I am 17 years old as of writing this, so my political and philosophical journey is far from over, but so far I am happy with the intellectual progress that i've made through my few years of gaining political literacy, and I feel like its an important enough part of my personality for me to discuss it in one of the pages on this site.

I will start by clarifying that I am a marxist, black liberationist, queer liberationist, gender accelerationist, and overall extreme progressive. However, I don't prescribe myself to any specific ideological tendency, and I think people who do are often uneducated on the deeper political, philosophical, and historical intricacies that contribute to the specifics of an ideology or tendency. I naturally found myself more attracted to certain concepts, thinkers, philosophies, positions, etc. that lead me to the positions that I hold now, and althought I am not familiar with all of the theory and history and philosophy of everything that led me to the views I have now, I will say that I feel very comfortable describing and defending them, moreso than I did in the past when I had prescribed myself to ideological tendencies. A humorous, but rough, summarized description of my beliefs could be expressed as follows:

"Deleuzo-Leninist Marxism with Damenite and Maoist characteristics"
